About Me
I'm the Co-Founder & CEO of Kritikos — the AI data layer for K-12 schools. We connect fragmented school software (SIS, LMS, and IEP platforms) and deliver proactive daily action plans to teachers and students via SMS and voice. No new dashboard. No new login.
Currently studying Economics & Data Science at UC Berkeley (Class of 2027), with 4+ years of experience across the education technology ecosystem spanning product, strategy, operations, and venture capital.

What I'm Building
🏗️ Kritikos — AI-native data infrastructure for schools. Aggregates Canvas, PowerSchool, Google Classroom, and more into a single unified thread delivered over text and voice. Currently in private beta and live in schools.
📚 Previously: Co-Founded Moonshot Mentors (acquired) — a mentorship platform for students in entrepreneurship and technology.
